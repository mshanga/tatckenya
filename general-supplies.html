GENERAL SUPPLIES

It is an accepted principle of good business to concentrate on your core business and let other specialists handle the peripheral issues. As such the practice of outsourcing has taken root in modern business.

For timely, quality and competitive supplies it is prudent for one to provide their requirements to general supplies specialists and let them provide the best quotes and ultimately benefit from their expertise.

This allows one to concentrate on what is important to them: growing and succeeding in their business.

We at Trans-Atlantic specialize in offering world class general supplies services. Contact us for all your general supplies needs.

1. Our Customers

We provide general supplies to a wide range of corporate bodies and entities in the private as well as public sectors including International Organisations. With technical and financial scalable capacity, we are capable to meet your needs efficiently and timely; providing you with both quality and value.

2. Our Strategy

Our key strategy is promptness in responding to our clients' needs. Our experienced team is keen to respond to your requirements with timeliness and attention to detail so that your expectations are met. Our objective is to provide competitive supreme services with a view to establish enduring and beneficial partnerships.

3. What We Supply

Our range of general supplies includes but not limited to chemicals, cereals, food processing raw materials, linen, and office supplies & equipment. With our direct links to either manufacturers or importers, depending on the products and goods required, you can be assured of timely delivery of quality supplies at very competitive prices.

4. Our Undertaking

Our mission is to provide timely general supplies solutions to our valued partners at competitive prices founded on innovativeness, efficiency and probity providing both quality and value. We assure you of personalized service where all your concerns are addressed. Come to us for timely deliveries, competitive pricing, and quality.
